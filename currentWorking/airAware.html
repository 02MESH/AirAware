Reading values

  • Temperature: The recommend range for temperature should be around 20°C-24°C, although you can adjust this in the Configuration page.
  • Humidity: The recommended range for humidity should be around 30%-60%. However, you can adjust this value as per your liking in the Configuration page.
  • Particulate Matter 2.5: PM2.5 Concentration below 10 µg/m³ has been considered healthy by the WHO. Values between 10 µg/m³ and 25 µg/m³ means that the air is slightly contaminated and the air purifier is needed to sanitize the air because of contaminants. A rating of above 25 µg/m³ means that the air is seriously contaminated which would also require turning on the air purifier, but also professional help if it doesn't improve.

Text Messages.

This feature isn't just about data monitoring; it's about proactive protection and control. Users can take prompt action upon receiving alerts, ensuring a safer and more comfortable environment. Furthermore, the system is thoughtfully designed to prevent excessive messaging. Instead of bombarding users with constant notifications, it smartly limits messages to one every 30 minutes for each breached threshold, avoiding unnecessary interruptions.
